Abstract

Problem

-

“Customer retention is a key challenge for companies across all industries.”

-Somebody
Understanding why customers leave and what keeps them engaged is essential for long-term success.

In the banking industry, churn is a persistent issue. Customers frequently close their accounts and switch to competitors. This turnover can lead to significant revenue loss for banks.

To tackle this problem, we analyzed a bank churn dataset. We leveraged machine learning to uncover the most telling predictors of customer retention. Our model provides data-driven insights to help banks proactively address churn and improve customer loyalty.

Our approach helps banks: Identify at-risk customers. Implement retention strategies based on insights.
